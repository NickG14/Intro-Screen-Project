Can I use your music in my Videogame?

If your game is free/non-commercial (no ads, in-game purchases, etc...) you are welcome to use any of my music against credit. If you are planning to sell your game or otherwise profit from it, please contact me and we can work on an agreement.

Songs published under Creative Commons "CC-BY" License can also be used in commercial video games. For a complete list of official releases and their licensing information, please take a look at my discography over at bandcamp.
